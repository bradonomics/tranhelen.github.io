Product Design Mentorship

Learn from an experienced mentor and jumpstart your career in design

Learn through practice

Work with a mentor 1:1 on assignments that will teach you the fundamentals of visual design and product design.

Build a portfolio

Design products that will eventually make up a portfolio you can use to get yourself into the door for a design job.

Apply actionable feedback

Receive fast and actionable feedback on how to improve your work tailored to your goals.

Practice your communication

Better understand how to work in a team by practicing how to present and receive feedback.

This mentorship can help

New Designers to the industry

If you need help starting a career in design, I can help introduce you to the craft and what to expect in the industry.

Designers looking to level up quickly

If you’re self-taught and have a few projects under your built, I can help refine your work and presentation.

Front-End Developers adding skills

Adding a Designer skillset to your repertoire can make you more desirable to employers and a better teammate.

You'll learn from someone currently working and teaching in the industry

My name is Helen Tran. I’m a formally trained Designer who has been building websites on the internet professionally for ~10 years now, the last two of which was spent leading product design at Shopify Toronto.

I have worked for all types of clients from start-ups to large corporations, in all types of environments from agency to product, working on various design projects from environmental to branding to digital.

In addition to my broad industry expertise, I’ve been teaching Design for the past 3 years. I love sharing what I’ve learned and introducing others to the industry.

You can find out more about me on my website.
