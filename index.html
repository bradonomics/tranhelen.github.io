Helen Tran

Designer / Entrepreneur

I’m a Designer who has worked with entrepreneurs my whole life. I enjoy helping others grow their businesses with a deliberate mindset.

Most recently, I was at Shopify for four years, the last two of which I spent as a Design Lead helping build out a team of 30 designers. While I was there, I taught design through Bloc part-time, spent a year publishing weekly articles about design and leadership, and was featured in several interviews and podcasts. I believe Design is a wonderful viewpoint from which to see the world and I want to make that viewpoint accessible.

I plan to continue building software for entrepreneurs. I believe an entrepreneur should never have to choose between what they love to do and their families so I intend to focus on eliminating time-sinks.

For fun, I write and compete with myself. I like to thru-hike long trails (my longest so far was the Kungsleden for 430km) and compete as an amateur fitness athlete. I live to learn and tend to fully commit myself to new interests out of curiousity.

I share my life with two succulents named Dante and Sedona. We live in Toronto because it’s objectively the best city in the world. Fire emoji.
